# CV Builder - Quick Reference Guide

## What's New

### 1. Professional Formatting
- **Font**: Times New Roman (serif font - professional standard)
- **Font Size**: 15px (equivalent to 11pt in MS Word for body text)
- **Line Height**: 1.5 (standard professional document spacing)
- **Colors**: Black text on white background (print-ready)

### 2. Block Format Structure
All sections follow strict block format:
- Text starts from the left margin (no indentation)
- Contact information on separate lines:
  ```
  John Doe

  john@email.com
  +1-234-567-8900
  New York, NY

  LinkedIn: https://linkedin.com/in/johndoe
  GitHub: https://github.com/johndoe
  ```

### 3. Enhanced Professional Summary (150+ Words)
The professional summary now includes:
- Opening statement about your professional identity
- Key skills and competencies you possess
- Details about your professional experience
- Your career goals and aspirations
- Personal qualities and work ethic
- Closing statement showing enthusiasm

**Example of detailed summary**:
*"Results-driven professional specializing in full-stack development, holding a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from [University]. I bring proven hands-on experience in professional environments where I have successfully delivered impactful projects and contributed to team objectives. In previous roles, I have demonstrated strong problem-solving abilities, effective communication, and the capacity to work collaboratively with diverse teams. I take pride in delivering quality work, meeting deadlines, and continuously seeking opportunities to improve processes and contribute to organizational success. I am particularly interested in pursuing opportunities in software development and cloud architecture, where I can leverage my existing skills while developing new expertise. I am passionate about continuous learning, innovation, and professional excellence, and thrive in collaborative, fast-paced environments that encourage creativity and growth. I am confident in my ability to make significant contributions to any organization and am excited about the opportunity to bring my skills, dedication, and enthusiasm to a dynamic team."*

### 4. Section Formatting

#### Contact Information
- Each contact detail on its own line
- Email, phone, address clearly separated
- Media links (LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.) displayed as:
  ```
  LinkedIn: https://linkedin.com/in/profile
  GitHub: https://github.com/profile
  ```

#### Skills & Expertise
- Grouped by proficiency level
- Displayed with bullet separators:
  ```
  SKILLS & EXPERTISE
  Advanced: Python, JavaScript, React • Intermediate: Node.js, SQL • Beginner: Docker, Kubernetes
  ```

#### Education
- Structured block format per entry:
  ```
  EDUCATION
  Bachelor of Science 2023
  University Name
  Computer Science
  ```

#### Employment History
- Structured block format per position:
  ```
  EMPLOYMENT HISTORY
  Senior Developer 2022 - Present
  Tech Company Inc
  Led development of customer-facing features, mentored junior developers, improved system performance by 40%.
  ```

#### Key Strengths
- Displayed as bullet points from margin:
  ```
  KEY STRENGTHS
  • Problem Solving & Analytical Thinking
  • Collaboration & Team Dynamics
  • Adaptability & Quick Learning
  ```

### 5. Print & PDF Quality
- Export to PDF with Times New Roman preserved
- A4 paper size, portrait orientation
- 0.5-inch margins on all sides
- Print-ready quality for professional submissions
- ATS (Applicant Tracking System) compatible

## How to Use CV Builder

### Step 1: Access
- Go to your Student Dashboard
- Click "CV Builder" button

### Step 2: Review
- System loads your profile data
- Professional summary auto-generated (150+ words)
- All sections populated with your information

### Step 3: Preview
- Check the CV preview
- Ensure all information is accurate
- Verify formatting looks professional

### Step 4: Download
- Click "Download CV as PDF"
- File saves with your name as filename
- Ready to share with employers
